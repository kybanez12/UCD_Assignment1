Where I be

Hometown

I was born an raised in the south eastern suburbs, primarily in Springvale. Over recent years have seen Springvale grow into a hub for the asian community. Littered with asian markets and restaurants, people travel from across the city to have a taste of what Springvale has to offer. You can't say no to a bowl of pho!

Greatest achievement

The greatest achievement of my life would have to be snowboarding in Japan. To be more specific, snowboarding a Triple Diamond Black run at Niseko, Japan. By far the greatest moment of my life.
